What the Numbers Mean

If 1,000 people see an SBW post this month →
~400 follow or save it →
~150 visit the Shopify store →
~50 add something to cart →
~20 complete a purchase →
~5–8 become repeat buyers who refer others.

This is why reach matters at the top and conversion matters at the bottom. Growing your audience and patching the leaks both move the needle.

Stage 01 · Top of Funnel
Awareness
Someone discovers SBW Co. for the first time. They weren't looking for you — you appeared in their world. This is the widest part of the funnel. Your only job here is to make a strong enough impression that they want to see more.
How People Discover SBW
📱
Instagram Reels — the single highest-reach format. A well-made Reel can reach 10x your follower count through the Explore page.
#️⃣
Hashtags — #austinfashion, #sbwco, #graphicapparel pull in people already interested in what you sell.
🔁
Shares & Tags — when someone reposts your content or tags a friend, their entire network sees SBW for the first time.
🤝
Collaborations — appearing on another Austin creator's page puts SBW in front of a warm, pre-qualified audience instantly.
📍
Location Tags — tagging Austin locations on posts gets you discovered by people searching those places.
Your Awareness Metrics to Watch
Reach
Unique accounts who saw a post
Impressions
Total times content was shown
What Works at This Stage
Reels over static posts · Strong visual hooks in first 2 seconds · Austin location tags · Consistent posting schedule · Trending audio on Reels · Bold, scroll-stopping imagery
⚠️ Common Leak
Posting inconsistently kills awareness. The algorithm stops showing your content to new people if you go quiet. Even one post per day keeps the pipeline full.
Stage 02 · Upper-Mid Funnel
Interest
They followed you, saved a post, or watched your Reel twice. They're curious but not committed. They're asking "is this brand for me?" Your content, your voice, and your story are what keep them here.
What Builds Interest for SBW
🎨
Design process content — showing the work builds credibility and curiosity simultaneously. Nobody else is showing this.
🖤
Founder story posts — people follow people, not logos. Wesley building SBW from scratch in Austin is a compelling story. Tell it repeatedly in different ways.
💬
Engaging captions — long, honest captions that make people feel something keep followers from unfollowing. Your kit already has these.
📊
Stories daily — story viewers are your warmest audience. They're choosing to tap through your content. These are your most interested followers.
🗳️
Polls and questions — colorway votes, design feedback, brand decisions made publicly. Participation creates investment in the brand's success.
Metrics to Watch
Saves
Strongest interest signal on IG
Story Views
Your warmest audience size
⚠️ Common Leak
Posting only product content without personality. People need to know who Wesley is before they trust SBW enough to visit the store. Balance brand posts with founder posts.
✓ The Fix
For every 2 product/brand posts, post 1 personal founder post. This ratio keeps the brand human and builds the emotional connection that converts followers into buyers.
Stage 03 · Mid Funnel
Consideration
They tapped your link in bio and landed on the Shopify store. They're looking at product photos, reading descriptions, checking prices. They're seriously thinking about buying. This is where most small brands lose people — not because the product is bad but because the store doesn't convert.
What Your Shopify Store Must Have
📸
Multiple product photos — front, back, close-up of graphic, lifestyle shot on a person, and a flat lay. People need to see the product from every angle before spending $65+.
✍️
Compelling descriptions — not just size and material. Tell the story of the design. What inspired it. What it feels like. Why it was made. Emotion sells.
⭐
Reviews visible — even 3–5 reviews dramatically increase conversion. This is why getting your first buyers to review is critical. Install a free Shopify review app.
📦
Clear shipping info — how long, how much, what's the return policy. Uncertainty at this stage kills purchases. Answer every question before they have to ask.
🔒
Trust signals — secure checkout badge, clear contact info, a real About page with Wesley's face and story. Trust is everything at $65–$110 price points.
📱
Mobile-optimized — almost every SBW customer will shop on their phone. Test every page on mobile before launch. Buttons must be easy to tap, text must be readable.
⚠️ The Biggest Leak in Most Small Brands
A beautiful Instagram driving traffic to a generic, unfinished Shopify store. The store must match the brand's quality. If the Instagram feels premium but the store feels unfinished, people leave.
✓ The Fix
Spend as much time on your Shopify store as you do on your Instagram. Every product page is a landing page. Treat it like one.
Stage 04 · Lower-Mid Funnel
Intent
They added to cart. This is a very strong signal — they want it. But cart abandonment rates average 70% across e-commerce. Most people add to cart and never come back. Your job here is to close the gap between wanting and buying.
What Converts Intent to Purchase
⚡
Frictionless checkout — every extra step loses customers. Enable Shop Pay and Apple Pay on Shopify. One-tap checkout converts dramatically better than multi-step forms.
📧
Abandoned cart email — Shopify sends this automatically if you collect emails. A simple "you left something behind" email with a photo of the product recovers 5–15% of abandoned carts.
⏰
Urgency — honestly used — "Only 8 left in this size" or "Drop 1 closes [date]" creates real urgency when true. Never fake scarcity — it destroys trust permanently.
🎁
Free shipping threshold — "Free shipping on orders over $80" nudges people to add a second item rather than abandon. Increases average order value and reduces cart abandonment.
Metrics to Watch
Cart Rate
% of store visitors who add to cart
Abandonment
% who add to cart but don't buy
⚠️ Common Leak
No email collection means abandoned carts are gone forever. Set up Klaviyo or Shopify Email from day one to capture emails and send the abandoned cart sequence automatically.
Stage 05 · Conversion
Purchase
They bought. The funnel worked. But what happens in the 30 minutes, 24 hours, and 7 days after the purchase determines whether they ever buy again — and whether they tell anyone about it.
The Post-Purchase Experience
📧
Order confirmation email — Shopify sends this automatically. Customize it with your brand voice. Add a personal note from Wesley. Make them feel like they made a great decision.
📦
Shipping confirmation — personalize this too. "Your SBW piece is on its way 🖤" feels very different from a generic tracking email. Both land in the same inbox — one builds a brand.
🎁
Packaging as branding — a tissue paper wrap, a sticker, a handwritten thank-you card costs almost nothing but creates a moment. That moment gets photographed and posted. Free marketing.
⭐
Review request email — send 7 days after delivery. Keep it personal: "Hey — I'm Wesley, I made this myself. An honest review means everything to a one-person brand." Real ask, real results.
📱
Tag request — include a card in the package: "Tag @sbwco when you wear it — I repost every single one." Turns customers into content creators for free.
Metrics to Watch
Conv. Rate
% of store visitors who buy
AOV
Average order value
Stage 06 · Bottom & Beyond
Loyalty & Advocacy
This is the most valuable and most neglected stage in small brand marketing. A loyal customer costs nothing to acquire — you already have them. An advocate is even more valuable — they send you new customers for free. The bottom of the funnel feeds the top.
Building Loyalty for SBW
🔁
Early access to Drop 2 — email past buyers first before the public announcement. "You were part of Drop 1 — you get first look at Drop 2." Exclusivity rewards loyalty.
📸
Repost customer photos — every time a customer tags SBW, repost it immediately. This validates the buyer, encourages others to do the same, and creates authentic brand content.
💌
Personal DMs — message new customers directly. "Just saw your order come through — thank you genuinely. Let me know how it fits." Nobody does this. It's unforgettable.
🎁
Surprise upgrades — occasionally include an extra sticker, a thank you card, or a small freebie in repeat orders. Costs almost nothing. Creates lifelong brand loyalty.
🌐
Community building — over time, SBW buyers become a community. A Discord, a close friends list on Instagram, a VIP email segment. The brand becomes a belonging, not just a purchase.
Metrics to Watch
Repeat %
% of customers who buy again
LTV
Lifetime value per customer
✓ The Compounding Effect
A customer who buys twice is worth 3x a one-time buyer. A customer who refers one friend doubles their value again. The math of loyalty compounds faster than any ad spend. Invest here first.
