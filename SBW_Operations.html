Operations · Taxes · Austin TX
The One Rule

Never mix personal and business money. Personal and business money in the same account makes taxes a nightmare, makes financials unreadable to investors, and can pierce your LLC's legal protection.

3-Account Structure
🏦
Business Checking (Mercury)
All SBW revenue in. All business expenses out. Pay yourself an owner's draw monthly. Free at mercuryhq.com.
💰
Tax Fund Savings
25–30% of every dollar earned. Never touch it. Marcus or Ally — 4–5% APY so your tax money earns interest while waiting.
👤
Personal Checking (Existing)
Receive your monthly owner's draw here. Living expenses come from this account only. Clean separation.
20-Minute Monthly Bookkeeping Routine
1.
Categorize all transactions in Wave Accounting
2.
Run Profit & Loss report — compare to financial model
3.
Transfer this month's tax set-aside to Tax Fund
4.
Pay yourself an owner's draw if the business earned it
5.
Note unusual expenses for your CPA
