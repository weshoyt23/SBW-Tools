Monthly Arc

Week 1 — Establish the Brand. Who is SBW Co.? What does it stand for? What's the aesthetic? Plant the flag hard.
Week 2 — Show the Work. Design process, inspiration, the craft behind the brand. Build credibility and curiosity.
Week 3 — Build Anticipation. Tease Drop 1 relentlessly. Reveal details slowly. Make people feel like insiders.
Week 4 — Drive to Launch. Countdown, strongest visuals, clear CTAs. Month ends with everyone waiting for Drop 1.
Days 29–30 — Close and Ignite Month 2. Reflect, thank the early community, announce what's coming.
