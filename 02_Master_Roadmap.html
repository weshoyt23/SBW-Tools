Week 1
Launch Everything
🐾 Dog Sitting

Complete and publish your Rover profile fully

Post your Nextdoor intro — your fastest first booking

Post Day 1–3 Instagram captions from your kit

Offer a discounted first booking to a neighbor or friend's dog — you need that first review

Set up Wag as a backup platform

💼 SBW Business

Purchase your domain and set up Shopify account

Sketch all 5 initial designs — rough concepts only

Set up Printify and connect to Shopify store

Set up business email and Adobe CC workspace

💰 Freelance

Build Fiverr profile with 2–3 of your best design samples

DM 5 small Austin businesses offering logo or graphic work

Week 1 Goal

Rover profile live. Instagram active. First booking attempt made. Everything else is a bonus.

Week 2
First Money In
🐾 Dog Sitting

Follow up on all Rover inquiries within minutes — speed wins bookings

Book your first meet & greet — even if free

Post your $14 walk special ad — already written in your kit

Post Instagram Days 4–7 from your content kit

Add a Story every day — availability, park walks, behind the scenes

💼 SBW Business

Digitize designs #1 and #2 in Adobe

Build Shopify store pages — Home, Shop, About, Contact

💰 Freelance

Follow up on Week 1 DMs to local businesses

Pitch 5 more businesses — restaurants, boutiques, barbers work well

Week 2 Goal

First Rover booking confirmed. First walk completed. At least 1 freelance conversation started. First dollar earned.

Week 3
Build the Machine
🐾 Dog Sitting

Aim for 2–3 walk clients this week — ask Week 2 clients to rebook

Ask your first client for a Rover review — do this right after the booking

Weekend: try to fill both days with boarding or walks

Post your best dog photo yet — the photographer angle post from your kit

💼 SBW Business

Digitize designs #3, #4, #5

Upload first 2 products as drafts on Shopify

Write product descriptions for tee and crewneck

Order 1–2 POD samples to check quality before launch

💰 Freelance

Land your first paid freelance design client — even a small logo job counts

Deliver exceptional work and ask for a Fiverr review

Week 3 Goal

First Rover review earned. 3+ walk clients. First freelance job delivered. Store taking shape.

Week 4
Review & Level Up
🐾 Dog Sitting

Review your Rover stats — which service is booking most?

Raise your walk price back to $20 now that you have reviews

Try to land your first repeat client — regulars are the goal

Target a fully booked weekend — both days earning

💼 SBW Business

All 5 designs complete and uploaded to Shopify as drafts

POD samples received — review quality, adjust if needed

Set your official store launch date — aim for end of Month 2

Start teasing SBW on Instagram — "something coming soon" content

💰 Month Review

Add up all income earned in Month 1 — dog sitting + freelance

Review expenses vs income — are you covering basics?

Decide: is Airbnb room rental needed or is income sufficient?

Write down 3 things that worked and 1 thing to change in Month 2

End of Month 1 Target

$400–$800 earned. At least 2 Rover reviews. Store 80% ready. First freelance client delivered. Momentum is real.
