WHAT TO CAPTURE FOR YOUR CASE STUDY

  • Screenshot Instagram Insights every Sunday — saves and reach are the key metrics
  • Record store analytics weekly even before launch (traffic sources, bounce rate)
  • Note every experiment: what you tested, why, and what happened
  • Save your best-performing posts with their stats for the portfolio gallery
  • Document the funnel at 30, 60, and 90 days — that arc is the story
  • First sale screenshot is portfolio gold — capture the moment
  • Email list milestones (first 50, 100, 250) show pre-launch demand
  • Saves-to-followers ratio matters more than follower count alone

HOW THIS BECOMES A PORTFOLIO PIECE

Your case study arc: Problem → Strategy → Execution → Results. The data you log here gives you the "Results" section with real numbers. The weekly notes give you the "What I Learned" narrative. Together they show strategic thinking plus execution — exactly what creative directors and marketing teams hire for.
