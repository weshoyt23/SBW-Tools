30-Day Brand Arc

Week 1 — Establish. Plant the flag, introduce Wesley, show the aesthetic.
Week 2 — Show the work. Design process, behind the scenes, quality proof.
Week 3 — Build anticipation. Teases, polls, sample reveal, drop date.
Week 4 — Drive to launch. Reveals, manifesto, hype, Drop 1 live.

Review Strategy
Ask in person at drop-off — eye contact and a genuine ask converts 3x better than the app's review request email.

Follow-up text within 2 hours: "Hey! [Dog name] was such a joy. If you have a spare moment, an honest Rover review would genuinely help me so much. Here's the direct link: [link]"

Over-deliver every time — more photos than expected, home early, a note about their dog's day. This creates reviews without even asking.

Respond publicly to every review — shows future clients you're attentive and communicative.

How SBW's 6-Stage Funnel Works
People move from discovering SBW to becoming loyal buyers through 6 stages. Each stage has a goal, a leak to watch, and a metric that tells you if it's working.
1
Awareness
How people find SBW — Instagram Reels, hashtags, Austin location tags, shares. 100% of the audience lives here.
Key metric: Reach (unique accounts who saw a post)
Common leak: Posting inconsistently. Algorithm stops showing you to new people.
2
Interest (~40%)
Founder story posts, design process, long honest captions, polls. People start following and saving posts.
Key metric: Saves (strongest Instagram interest signal)
Common leak: Only posting product — no personality. Need 2:1 brand to personal ratio.
3
Consideration (~15%)
Shopify store visit. Product photos, story-driven descriptions, visible reviews, clear return policy.
Key metric: Store session time and product page views
Common leak: Beautiful Instagram → unfinished store. Must match brand quality.
4
Intent (~5%)
Add to cart. Shop Pay and Apple Pay enabled. Abandoned cart email active. Honest scarcity messaging.
Key metric: Cart abandonment rate (industry avg: 70%)
Common leak: Checkout friction. Test mobile checkout personally.
5
Purchase (~2%)
Order placed. Personal confirmation email. Branded packaging. Review request at Day 7. Tag request in every package.
Key metric: Conversion rate (target 1–3%)
6
Loyalty (∞)
Repeat buyers. Customer photos reposted. Early drop access. Personal DM to every first-time buyer.
Key insight: A buyer who buys twice is worth 3x a one-time buyer.
